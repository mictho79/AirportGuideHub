London Stansted Airport Guide (STN) – London

IATA: STN ICAO: EGSS London, UK 1 Terminal

Airport Overview

London Stansted Airport (STN) is the fourth-busiest airport in the UK, handling approximately 28 million passengers annually, and serves as the primary base for Ryanair — Europe's largest low-cost carrier — as well as a major hub for easyJet and Jet2. Located approximately 55 km northeast of central London in Essex, near the town of Stansted Mountfitchet, the airport features one of the most architecturally celebrated terminal buildings in Europe, designed by Sir Norman Foster and opened in 1991. Its single-terminal design with all facilities at the same level creates an efficient and passenger-friendly layout for what is predominantly a point-to-point low-cost traffic airport.

Terminals

London Stansted operates a single terminal building with two distinct areas:

  • Main Terminal (Departures): All check-in desks, security, and landside facilities are in the main terminal building. The terminal is split into the landside check-in area and the airside departure zone.
  • Satellite Pier (Airside): After security, passengers access gates via an underground transit system linking the main terminal to two satellite pier buildings (the "Forest" and "Park" concourses). Each pier has gates numbered in different ranges.
💡 All gates are in the satellite building, reached by a short underground transit. Allow 10–15 minutes from security to gates, and note that gate distances vary — check the transit time to your specific gate on the departure boards.

WiFi Availability

Free WiFi is available throughout Stansted Airport under the network "Stansted Airport WiFi". The initial free tier provides 60 minutes of connectivity. For extended or unlimited access, a paid premium option is available. Signal coverage is strong throughout the terminal building and satellite piers. Many passengers use mobile data via UK/EU roaming as an alternative for longer layovers given the limited free time allocation.

Transport to City Center

Stansted Express Train

The Stansted Express is the primary and fastest link, running non-stop to London Liverpool Street Station in approximately 47 minutes. Trains run every 15–30 minutes and operate from approximately 05:00 to 00:30. Standard fares vary by time of booking — advance online tickets can be as low as £10.50, while on-the-day tickets can cost £27–31. The service is operated by Greater Anglia and departs from the basement level of the terminal building (follow signs to the railway station).

Bus Services (National Express and FlixBus)

National Express coaches run 24 hours to London Victoria Coach Station (approximately 75–90 minutes, from £6) and to various other London and UK destinations. FlixBus offers budget coach services to London Liverpool Street and other stops. Buses depart from the ground floor bus station outside the terminal arrivals area.

Taxi and Rideshare

Black cabs and licensed taxis are available outside arrivals. The fare to central London is approximately £80–100 and takes 60–90 minutes depending on traffic on the M11. Uber operates at Stansted from a dedicated pickup zone. Bolt and FREE NOW are also available.

Airport Lounges

  • No1 Lounge Stansted (Airside): The main independent lounge accessible via Priority Pass and day pass, located airside before the transit to satellite piers. Offers a full buffet, cocktail bar, showers, and quiet work space.
  • Escape Lounge Stansted: Another independent pay-per-use and Priority Pass lounge with comfortable seating, hot food, and a well-stocked bar, also located in the main departure zone airside.
  • Aspire Lounge Stansted: A third option for Priority Pass holders with buffet food, wines, and business amenities in the airside area.
  • Ryanair Business Plus Lounge: Accessible for Ryanair Business Plus fare holders, offering a separate seating area with priority boarding access and light refreshments.
  • Jet2holidays Premium Lounge: For Jet2holidays and Jet2 premium passengers on leisure routes, offering drinks and light snacks.

Hotels Near the Airport

  • Radisson Blu Hotel London Stansted Airport: Directly connected to the terminal by an internal walkway, offering 499 rooms, an indoor pool, spa, and multiple dining outlets.
  • Holiday Inn Express London Stansted Airport: Located 5 minutes by complimentary shuttle from arrivals, a popular budget-friendly option with 24-hour reception.
  • Hilton Garden Inn Stansted Airport: A modern 3-star hotel 3 km from the terminal with complimentary shuttle and comfortable rooms.
  • Premier Inn Stansted Airport: A reliable budget option 2 km from the terminal with the brand's famous "Good Night Guarantee" and consistent quality.
  • Travelodge Stansted Airport: The most affordable on-airport accommodation option, attached to the terminal complex and popular with early-morning departures.

Food and Restaurants

  • Jamie's Deli (Airside): A casual Italian-inspired food concept by Jamie Oliver, offering fresh pasta, antipasti, and Italian sandwiches in the airside departure area.
  • Wagamama (Airside): The popular UK-Japanese noodle restaurant serving ramen, katsu curry, and gyoza — ideal for a filling pre-flight meal.
  • Wetherspoons (The Gateway to the World): A classic British pub serving full English breakfasts, pub meals, and a wide selection of ales and ciders at very reasonable prices.
  • Leon (Airside): A UK natural fast food chain with wraps, rice boxes, and superfood salads focusing on healthy, fresh ingredients.
  • Costa Coffee and Pret a Manger: Both available throughout the terminal for coffees and quick snacks, with multiple locations landside and airside.
  • Burger King and McDonald's: Available in the departure zone for familiar options at accessible prices.

Travel Tips

  • Book Stansted Express tickets in advance online for the best fares — last-minute tickets at the station can be nearly three times the advance price.
  • Stansted is a very busy budget airline airport — queues at check-in and security can be very long for Ryanair flights; use web check-in and arrive at least 2 hours before departure.
  • The satellite piers require a transit journey underground — factor in 10–15 minutes from security to your gate, and more if your gate is far along the satellite.
  • The free WiFi is limited to 60 minutes — download any content you need before leaving home, or consider purchasing the all-day WiFi pass.
  • Currency exchange at the airport offers poor rates — withdraw GBP from ATMs or use a fee-free travel card (Monzo, Starling, Revolut) for better rates.
  • Stansted's duty-free zone is surprisingly well-stocked with premium spirits, fragrances, and cosmetics at competitive prices.
  • For late-night or early-morning flights, the Radisson Blu connected to the terminal is the most convenient option — book in advance as prices rise sharply near the stay date.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get from Stansted Airport to central London?

The Stansted Express train to Liverpool Street Station is the fastest option at approximately 47 minutes, with advance tickets from £10.50. National Express coaches to Victoria Coach Station take 75–90 minutes and can cost as little as £6 when booked in advance. Taxis to central London cost £80–100.

How far is Stansted Airport from London?

London Stansted is approximately 55 km northeast of central London, located in Essex near the town of Stansted Mountfitchet. While further from central London than Heathrow or Gatwick, the direct Stansted Express train makes the journey to Liverpool Street in just 47 minutes.

Which airlines fly from London Stansted?

Ryanair is by far the dominant carrier at Stansted, operating hundreds of European routes from its UK primary base. easyJet, Jet2, Wizz Air, and Loganair also operate from STN. The airport is primarily focused on European leisure and short-haul point-to-point routes.

Is there a hotel at Stansted Airport?

Yes, the Radisson Blu Hotel is directly connected to the terminal building via a covered walkway and is the most convenient on-site option. The Travelodge is also within the airport complex. Other hotels in the immediate vicinity offer shuttle services to the terminal.

Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport Guide (CGO) – Zhengzhou

IATA: CGO ICAO: ZHCC Zhengzhou, China 2 Terminals

Airport Overview

Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport (CGO) serves Zhengzhou, the capital of Henan Province and one of China's fastest-growing logistics and manufacturing hubs, located approximately 38 km east of the city centre in Xinzheng. The airport handled over 29 million passengers in 2019 and is particularly notable as a major cargo hub — Zhengzhou has emerged as a critical node in global supply chains, especially for iPhone manufacturing by Foxconn. Zhengzhou Aviation Economy Zone surrounding the airport is one of China's national aviation economic development zones.

Terminals

  • Terminal 2 (T2): The primary passenger terminal handling domestic and most international flights. China Southern Airlines, Air China, and the majority of carriers operate from here.
  • Terminal 1 (T1): Legacy terminal still handling some domestic traffic during peak periods and some charter operations.
💡 Terminal 2 is the main hub at CGO and where most passengers will depart and arrive. The cargo operations at Zhengzhou are world-scale — you'll see a significant cargo aircraft presence on the apron. Allow extra time during peak periods as the airport processes very high cargo and passenger volumes simultaneously.

WiFi Availability

Free Wi-Fi is available at CGO under the network "CGO_Airport_WiFi". Registration via SMS with a Chinese mobile number is required. International travellers should purchase a Chinese SIM card at the airport for both Wi-Fi registration and local connectivity. China's Great Firewall restricts Google, WhatsApp, Facebook, and other Western services — a VPN installed before arrival is necessary.

Transport to City Centre

Airport Intercity Railway

The Zhengzhou Airport Intercity Railway (Urban Rail Line 2 extended) connects CGO to Zhengzhou's central railway station and city metro network. The journey to Zhengzhou Railway Station takes approximately 30–35 minutes. Fares are CNY 15–20. Trains operate from 06:30 to 23:00 with 10-minute peak frequency.

Bus Services

Multiple express bus lines serve key Zhengzhou destinations including the High-Speed Railway Station, East Bus Terminal, and major business districts. Journey times are 40–70 minutes with fares of CNY 15–25.

Taxi and Rideshare

Taxis are available from the rank outside T2 arrivals. The journey to central Zhengzhou takes 35–50 minutes and costs CNY 80–120. DiDi is widely available and recommended for transparent pricing.

Airport Lounges

CGO has multiple lounge options in T2 for domestic and international passengers.

  • China Southern VIP Lounge (T2): Business class lounge with hot buffet, noodle station, and beverages; accessible to SkyTeam Elite Plus members.
  • Air China VIP Lounge (T2): Star Alliance Gold accessible lounge with Chinese hot buffet and comfortable seating.
  • CIP First Class Lounge (T2, International): Pay-per-use lounge available to all passengers with full dining and shower access.
  • Longmen Lounge (T2): Named after the famous Longmen Grottoes, this VIP lounge features traditional Henan cultural décor and pay-per-use access for all travellers.

Hotels Near the Airport

The Zhengzhou Aviation Economy Zone and surrounding Xinzheng area have a growing number of hotels catering to the airport's large business traveller population.

  • Zhengzhou Airport Hotel (on-site): Connected to T2, offering 24-hour service and transit-friendly packages for layover passengers.
  • Kempinski Hotel Zhengzhou: Approximately 15 km from the airport in the business district with luxury facilities and multiple restaurants.
  • Sheraton Zhengzhou Hotel: Approximately 30 km from the airport in the city centre, popular with business travellers visiting Zhengzhou's trade fairs.
  • Hampton Inn Zhengzhou Airport: Budget-friendly option approximately 2 km from T2 with free shuttle service and complimentary breakfast.
  • Marriott Zhengzhou: Full-service option approximately 25 km from the airport in the Zhengdong New District financial area.

Food and Restaurants

Zhengzhou Airport's T2 offers a mix of Henan regional cuisine and national chains.

  • Henan Hui Noodles Counter (T2): Serving Zhengzhou's famous huimian noodles — flat mutton noodle soup — a local comfort food staple.
  • McDonald's (T2): Full menu available with China-specific items; conveniently located near departure gates.
  • KFC (T2): Popular with domestic travellers; China-market menu items available.
  • Starbucks (T2): Standard menu with China-exclusive seasonal drinks available.
  • Luckin Coffee (T2): Domestic coffee chain offering affordable lattes and freshly baked snacks.
  • Shaolin Noodle House (T2): Restaurant themed around the nearby Shaolin Temple, serving Henan staples and kung fu cultural references.

Travel Tips

  • Zhengzhou is the gateway to the Shaolin Temple (birthplace of kung fu), Longmen Grottoes (UNESCO World Heritage Site), and the Yellow River — consider a day trip from the city.
  • The airport is a major cargo hub for global electronics; you'll notice a very high density of FedEx, UPS, and Cainiao cargo operations.
  • Install DiDi and a VPN before arriving in China; both are essential for navigating Zhengzhou independently.
  • China's 144-hour visa-free transit policy applies to Zhengzhou for eligible nationalities; verify before travel.
  • WeChat Pay and Alipay are near-universal payment methods; link a foreign card to Alipay before travel.
  • Zhengzhou's climate is temperate continental — summers are hot (35°C+), winters are cold; the best travel period is April–May or September–October.
  • The airport intercity rail is fast, affordable, and comfortable — the preferred option for most city-bound travellers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get from Zhengzhou Airport to the city centre?

The Airport Intercity Railway is the best option, reaching Zhengzhou Railway Station in about 30–35 minutes for CNY 15–20. Taxis and DiDi take 35–50 minutes and cost CNY 80–120. Airport buses cost CNY 15–25 and take 40–70 minutes depending on the destination.

Why is Zhengzhou Airport a major cargo hub?

Zhengzhou is home to Foxconn's largest iPhone manufacturing plant and other major electronics suppliers. The airport's proximity to this manufacturing base, combined with government investment in the Zhengzhou Aviation Economy Zone and preferential freight policies, has made CGO one of the busiest cargo airports in China by cargo volume.

Can I visit the Shaolin Temple from Zhengzhou Airport?

Yes, the Shaolin Temple is approximately 80 km southwest of the airport (about 1.5 hours by taxi or bus). From Zhengzhou city centre, high-speed trains run to Dengfeng area regularly. With a 144-hour visa-free transit, a same-day Shaolin Temple visit is feasible.

Does Zhengzhou Airport have Priority Pass lounges?

CGO has pay-per-use CIP lounges and designated airline lounges in T2. Priority Pass is accepted at selected lounge facilities; confirm at the lounge reception desk as access partnerships may vary. Airline lounges require business class tickets or relevant status cards.
